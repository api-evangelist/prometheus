Continuing our series of interviews with users of Prometheus, Tom Wilkie from Weaveworks talks about how they choose Prometheus and are now building on it. Can you tell us about Weaveworks? Weaveworks offers Weave Cloud , a service which "operationalizes" microservices through a combination of open source projects and software as a service.
