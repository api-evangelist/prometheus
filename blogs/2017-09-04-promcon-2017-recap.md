What happened Two weeks ago, Prometheus users and developers from all over the world came together in Munich for PromCon 2017 , the second conference around the Prometheus monitoring system. The purpose of this event was to exchange knowledge and best practices and build professional connections around monitoring with Prometheus. Google's Munich office offered us a much larger space this year, which allowed us to grow from 80 to 220 attendees while still selling out!
