Prometheus documentation now includes a new Kapa.ai integration. This is available as part of the partnership between CNCF and Kapa.ai , which helps CNCF projects make their documentation and knowledge more accessible. You can now use the Ask AI entry on prometheus.io to ask questions in natural language and get answers grounded in Prometheus documentation.
