Following the recent release of Prometheus 3.0 beta at PromCon in Berlin, the Prometheus Team is excited to announce the immediate availability of Prometheus Version 3.0! This latest version marks a significant milestone as it is the first major release in 7 years. Prometheus has come a long way in that time, evolving from a project for early adopters to becoming a standard part of the cloud native monitoring stack.
