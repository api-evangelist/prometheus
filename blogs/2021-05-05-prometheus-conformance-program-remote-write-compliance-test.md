As announced by CNCF and by ourselves , we're starting a Prometheus conformance program. To give everyone an overview of where the ecosystem is before running tests officially, we wanted to show off the newest addition to our happy little bunch of test suites: The Prometheus Remote Write compliance test suite tests the sender part of the Remote Write protocol against our specification . During Monday's PromCon , Tom Wilkie presented the test results from the time of recording a few weeks ago.
