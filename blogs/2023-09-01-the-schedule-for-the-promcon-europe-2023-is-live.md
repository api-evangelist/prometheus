PromCon Europe is the eighth conference fully dedicated to the Prometheus monitoring system Berlin, Germany – September 1, 2023 – The CNCF and the Prometheus team, released the two-day schedule for the single-track PromCon Europe 2023 conference happening in Berlin, Germany from September 28 to September 29, 2023. Attendees will be able to choose from 21 full-length (25min) sessions and up to 20 five-minute lightning talk sessions spanning diverse topics related to Prometheus .
